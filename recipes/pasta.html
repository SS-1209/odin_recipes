PASTA

let's make vegetable white sause pasta within 10 min .

ingredients

  • pasta(of your choice)
  • veggies
    • baby corn
    • onion
    • capsicum
    • brocculi
  • butter
  • cream
  • milk
  • garlic
  • flour
  • seasoning and cheese

steps to make pasta

  1. boil the pasta in one cup of water,add little oil and salt. drain the water and keep the pasta aside adter 5 min of boiling
  2. Melt 1 tablespoon of butter in a pan on medium heat. Add all the veggies
  3. Cook the veggies for 2 minutes. They should remain crunchy.
  4. Once done, remove veggies onto a plate.
  5. To another pan or you can use the same pan, now add 1 tablespoon olive oil and the remaining tablespoon of butter.
  6. Once the butter melts, add the 5-6 chopped garlic cloves and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  7. add in 1 & 1/2 tablespoons of flour.
  8. Whisk the flour continuously, using a wire whisk for around 30 to 60 seconds. You donâ€™t have to brown the flour much.
  9. Then add in 2 cups of milk. You can also add 1/4 cup heavy cream here or use additional 1/4 cup milk.
  10. Mix everything using a whisk so that there are no lumps formed.
  11. Add in the seasonings
  12. Mix in everything and let the sauce simmer.
  13. Sauce will thicken as it simmers and in 4 to 5 minutes it will coat the back of the spoon. Turn heat to lowest.
  14. Add in the veggies and the boiled pasta.
  15. Toss until the pasta and veggies are coated with the sauce. Taste test and adjust the seasonings at this point. Serve warm!
